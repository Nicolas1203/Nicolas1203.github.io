Hello there

Congrats for finding this website amongst all my homonyms. I am a PhD student, close to defending. Actually, I’m currently a teaching assistant while finishing my PhD. The exact date of my defense will be out soon, stay tuned if you are interested!

My scientific interests lie in Deep Learning and Computer Vision. More specifically Online Continual Learning, Representation Learning and Knowledge Distillation. You can find out all my publications on my Google Scholar. You can also find me on Linkedin and GitHub.

I will probably add more information about myself here but you get the big picture, probably.
